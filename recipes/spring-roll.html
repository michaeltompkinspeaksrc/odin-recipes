Spring Rolls

This is an amazing and simple dessert that takes no time to make!

Ingredients:

  • 8 spring roll wrappers
  • 4 med bananas cut in half
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon apple pie spice
  • vegetable oil
  • caramel sauce and vanilla ice cream
  1. Heat the oil. Pour about an inch of oil into a large frying pan. Heat to 350 F.
  2. Blend sugar mixture. In a small bowl blend the sugar and apple pie spice together.
  3. Wet the spring rolls. Place (individually) each spring roll in water for 10 seconds.
  4. Sprinkle with sugar. Sprinkle the spring roll wrapper with a teaspoon of the sugar mixture. Place tha banana onto the wrapper. Carefully fold in the sides, then roll-up.
  5. Fry the spring rolls. USing tongs, place the spring rolls into the hot oil. Cook for about 5 mintues, turning after 2 and a half mintues.
  6. Serve. Serve hot with a side of vanilla ice cream and caramel drizzled on top.
